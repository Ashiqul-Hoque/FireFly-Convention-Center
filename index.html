FireFly Convention Centre (FCC) is one of the ORIGINAL and LEADING convention centers in Bangladesh. It was established in 2010 with the goal of providing the highest standard of services in the Hospitality Industry.

Our establishment in perfect for both corporate and Social Events, Such events include, Annual General Meeting, Business exhibitions, Seminars, Wedding Events, Birthday Parties, Get Together or any other Indoor Events.

Spectra Convention Centre offers Luxurious Banquet Halls, Business Conference Rooms, Business Centre, all of which that are equipped with a modern hygienic Kitchen, car parking facilities and a fantastic and cozy location in the central of Dhaka.
